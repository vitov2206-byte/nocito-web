FAQ

Simple answers before you install.

Nocito is Android-first, privacy-first and designed to work without forcing you into bank sync or a cloud account.

Can I use Nocito offline?

Yes. You can capture expenses offline and they'll process automatically once you're back online — or enable local AI for 100% private, fully on-device tracking.

Do I need an account?

No. Nocito works without creating an account, on any tier.

Do I need to connect my bank?

No. Nocito does not use bank sync. It works from receipts, PDFs, voice notes and manual entries.

Where are my receipts stored?

Original images, PDFs and audio files are stored privately on your phone — not on any server.

What languages and formats are supported?

Nocito supports 12 languages, all world currencies, and both EU and US date and number formats.

Is Nocito available on iPhone?

Not yet. Nocito is available on Android now, with iOS planned for later.
